# #84 Skip Warrant

A psychological liberation tool for moms (and anyone) overwhelmed by daily chores. It provides "official" cosmic permission to stop working and start resting.

## Core Philosophy
- **1 Tool / 1 Action**: One button to receive an indisputable mandate to skip chores.
- **Decision Fatigue Killer**: Removes the burden of choice by outsourcing the "permission to rest" to the universe.
- **Physical Feedback**: Uses a 3-second "suspense" period followed by a screen-shaking digital stamp to provide a sense of official completion.

## Features
- **100 Cosmic Excuses**: A database of 100 absurd yet convincing reasons why you shouldn't do the dishes, laundry, or cleaning today.
- **Official Branding**: Each warrant comes with a unique serial number (e.g., #84-XXXXX) to enhance the sense of "authority."
- **Evidence Mode**: A dedicated "Use as Evidence" feature to copy the warrant text, allowing users to deflect chore requests from family members without guilt.
- **High-Impact UI**: A stark contrast between the minimalist Fridge Combo design and the bold, semi-transparent "APPROVED" stamp.

## How to Use
1. **The Limit**: Open the tool when you feel you've reached your mental or physical limit with housework.
2. **The Warrant**: Tap "ISSUE WARRANT" and wait for the 3-second planetary alignment check.
3. **The Stamp**: Once the "APPROVED" stamp hits the screen with a shake, your duties are officially cancelled for the day.
4. **The Evidence**: Use the copy link to send proof to family members if they ask, "What's for dinner?" (The answer is now: Takeout).

## Design System
- **Background**: #FFF / **Main**: #333
- **Accent/Toast**: #CCC (for visibility on dark backgrounds)
- **Stamp**: #FF4444 (Semi-transparent with a 15-degree rotation)
- **Radius**: 15px (Fridge Combo Standard)
